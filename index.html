Which ESO role should you play?

Like most MMOs, The Elder Scrolls Online has 3 primary roles: tank, healer, and damage dealer. Which one should you play?

1. Have you played an MMO before?

2. How do you react under stressful situations?

3. How do you like to fight enemies?

4. How good are you at fast hand movements and reactions?

5. How much time do you have to grind sets?

6. Do you prefer telling people what to do, or being told what to do?

7. Would you prefer being important to the group, or having room to mess up?
